The philosophy

A second skin is not an undergarment.
It is a ritual.

Most layers hide. Ours reveal — not the body, but the intention. Each piece is cut from natural fibres, finished with flat seams and zero visible branding. No unnecessary elastic, no synthetics where they don't belong.

Dariusz designs Second Skin for the hours when no one is watching. Because that is when true comfort becomes essential.

Material

Only what grows again

No polyester, no microplastics. Second Skin uses natural cotton — selected for softness, weight, and how it holds its shape after 60 washes. We tested multiple suppliers. We chose one.

The construction is what most brands skip: flat seams that don't press into skin, no tags, no logos on the garment itself. The label is on the packaging — not on you.

  • · Flat seams throughout — no pressure points
  • · No internal branding or tags
  • · Produced in Poland, shipped from Berlin
  • · Each piece numbered and registered

Built to last. Easy to keep.

Hand wash cold. Dry flat. Never tumble dry. Natural fibres soften with each wash — they improve, not degrade. Any issue with fit or condition: contact us directly.
