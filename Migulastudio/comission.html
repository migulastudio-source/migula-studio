How we work

The path to your artifact

First exchange
Virtual or in‑person, we discuss your vision, lifestyle, and material preferences.
02
Material & silhouette
Selection from rare fabrics, followed by a preliminary sketch and toile.

04
Hand‑finishing

Materials with memory

Only natural fibres that age with grace: hand‑loomed linens, Scottish wools, Japanese cottons, and raw silks. Each bolt is selected personally by Dariusz.

  • · Undyed, unbleached options
  • · Rare deadstock fabrics
  • · Bespoke dyeing on request

Provenance of craft

Every commission is logged in the Atelier Archive: the client’s name, the date, the fabric origin, and the hours of handwork. Your garment carries its own story.

  • · Signed & numbered
  • · Lifetime care advice
