Weight: 180 g/m² — the right side of the balance between structure and softness. Heavy enough to hold a clean silhouette and resist transparency; light enough to move with the body.

Composition: 95% cotton / 5% elastane as standard. The elastane allows the fabric to recover its shape after wearing — it doesn't stretch out across the chest or under the arms. 100% cotton available on request.

Fit: Straight. Not oversized, not structured. The garment rests on the body — it doesn't hang, it doesn't cling.

  • Machine wash 30°C / 86°F, inside out
  • Lay flat to dry — preserves shape and length
  • Do not tumble dry
  • Iron inside out on low heat if needed
  • Do not bleach

✧ Your measurements. Your pattern. ✧

Most garments made for fuller builds are simply cut wider — and they show it. They hang loose at the shoulder, the sleeve floats away from the arm, and the whole effect is shapeless.

Here it works differently: the waist and torso are widened to your measurements, but the shoulder width and sleeve circumference remain proportionate to your actual frame. No tent effect. The shoulder seam still ends at the shoulder.

Who is this for?
Men who carry more volume through the midsection but don't want shapeless T-shirts. Those who find standard L or XL too wide in the shoulders, but too tight across the belly.
