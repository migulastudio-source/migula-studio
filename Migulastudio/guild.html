The Migula Guild
Not a membership. A kinship.

A limited circle of clients who commission work through an ongoing dialogue with Dariusz. Twenty spots per cycle. No waiting list — only alignment.

Request consideration
Why the guild

More than a client

The Guild was born from a simple observation: the most meaningful garments emerge from repeated collaboration, not a single transaction. Dariusz established the Guild to work with individuals over years — not seasons.

Members receive first access to new fabrics, private viewings, and the ability to commission without repeating the introductory process. But the true value is quieter: a shared language of craft.

“I want to know how you live, not just what you wear.” — Dariusz Migula

20

Spots per annual cycle

The Guild is not a loyalty program. It is a conscious limitation. Dariusz accepts no more than twenty active members each year. When a spot opens, it is offered to those who have expressed genuine alignment — never through open advertisement.

No expiry

Once accepted, membership is ongoing. You are not required to commission every year. The relationship endures — a quiet understanding that when you are ready, the atelier is there.

Request an invitation

The Guild does not accept general applications. However, you may express your interest. Dariusz reviews each submission personally. If there is alignment, you will be invited to an introductory conversation.

Submissions are reviewed quarterly. Due to the limited number of spots, not all expressions receive a reply — but each is read.
